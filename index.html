How to Reset your Windows Password

Objective

To successfully reset your password in case you lost it.

Prerequisites

  • For option B, you will need access to an admin account on the same device..
  1. Press Crtl + Alt + Delete at the same time.
  2. Is going to take you to another window with four options, select Change Password.
  3. Now, it’s going to ask your old password once, and for your new password twice.

1. Computer Management

  • Log in to another admin account on your PC.
  • Press Win + X at the same time and click Computer Management .
  • In the left panel, go to: System Tools > Local Users and Groups > Users .
  • In the middle panel, find the account you want to reset.
  • Right-click the account > select Set Password.
  • Click Proceed on the warning window.
  • Enter a new password and comfirm it.
  • Click OK to save changes.
  • Log out and log into the original account using the new password.

Process Complete

Your new password should now be configured and ready for use.
